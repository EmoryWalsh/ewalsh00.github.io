The Perfect Banana Bread

Description

This is my favorite banana bread recipe. My mom and I have been using this for years.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 overripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • mini chocolate chips
  • large bowl
  • pan
  • whisk
  • spatula

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Butter or spray the pan thouroughly.
  2. Peel bananas into a bowl. Mash until smooth.
  3. Melt butter in a microwave. In a large bowl, combine the butter and sugar. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Whisk eggs, milk, and vanilla into batter.
  5. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir until no flour is visible.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Pour batter into pan and bake for 50-60 minutes.
